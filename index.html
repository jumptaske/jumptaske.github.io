JumpTask - Home Official Website

JumpTask is a gig economy platform that offers individuals the opportunity to earn money by completing simple microtasks. It was introduced in 2022 as a project that leverages blockchain technology and cryptocurrency to create a decentralized and transparent ecosystem for freelancers and businesses.

Key Facts about JumpTask:

User Base: As of December 2022, JumpTask had amassed over 1.84 million users worldwide. The platform's variety of microtasks and the advantages of blockchain technology contributed to this growth.

Decentralization of the Gig Economy: JumpTask aims to decentralize the gig economy by leveraging blockchain and smart contract technology. It simplifies the relationship between independent workers and businesses, making it more transparent and cost-efficient.

Business Model: JumpTask operates on a self-sustaining demand loop. Businesses submit tasks to the platform and buy JumpTokens ($JMPT) to pay freelancers for completing these jobs. Once a microtask is finished, JumpTokens are sent to the freelancer, creating a sustained demand cycle.

Cryptocurrency: JumpTask operates with its own cryptocurrency called JumpToken ($JMPT). It runs on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and can be traded on PancakeSwap. The team aims to get the token listed on centralized exchanges in the future.

Staking Options: The JumpTask platform offers staking options through "Fluid Staking." Users can stake their $JMPT in three pools: Starter, Gainer, and Pro, with varying minimum investment requirements and annual percentage reward rates.

Earning Opportunities: JumpTask provides users with multiple ways to earn. Freelancers can make money through games, surveys, and other microtasks. They can also earn passive income by sharing internet bandwidth and staking JumpTokens. The platform plans to add more ways to earn in the future.

Team Members: The JumpTask project is led by CEO Juozapas M. Preik≈°a, CBDO Adomas Ronkaitis, and CTO Justas Vasiliauskas. The team has experience in cryptocurrency investment, initial coin offerings, and app monetization.

Implementation Phases: JumpTask is being implemented in three phases. The first phase involves identifying new partners and supporting them through intermediation. The second phase focuses on creating a marketplace that gives partners more autonomy and users a wider selection of gig options. The final phase aims to decentralize the global gig economy using BSC smart contract templates.

Partnerships: JumpTask has established partnerships with various companies that supply microtasks to the platform. These partners include passive income apps like Honeygain, microtask providers like Offertoro, ayeT-Studios, AdGateMedia, and AdGem, as well as global online survey providers like in.Brain and CPX Research.

Community Engagement: JumpTask holds monthly "Ask Me Anything" (AMA) sessions to engage with the community. During these sessions, users can ask questions, participate, and collect digital NFT collectibles called POAPs (proof of attendance protocol).
